About Us

SNUG Baby Carriers was started by three young mothers from Norway in 2017. Our main goal with these products is to help new mothers to stay active and at the same time be close to their new baby. It is important for us that our carriers are comfortable, fashionable and safe.

Our vision is to be the preferred baby carrier supplier in Scandinavia.

All our products are handmade with the highest quality possible. We have a warranty of 5 years on our baby carriers.
